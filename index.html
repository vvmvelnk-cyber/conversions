STEEL HARDNESS CONVERSION T1

General Information

The hardness converters are given here for some groups of steels according to standard ISO 18265:2003(E). They are a good basis for a approximate comparison of hardness-to-hardnes of standard hardness testing methods and hardness-to-tensile strength.

Please note that this comparison cannot replace testing results.

For more details of hardness values conversion, see the latest edition of standard ISO 18265.

Instruction

Select appropriate steel group convertor, enter the hardness value by one test method or the tensile strength value, click the mouse or enter, then you will get the converted approximate values ​​by the other methods.

Standards and Links

Common standards for metal hardness and tensile strength testing:

  • Brinell test - EN ISO 6506 / ASTM E10
  • Vickers test - EN ISO 6507 / ASTM E92, ASTM E384
  • Rockwell test - EN ISO 6508 / ASTM E18
  • Tensile Strength test - EN ISO 6892 / ASTM E8
